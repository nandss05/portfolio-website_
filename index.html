Welcome to My Portfolio

Creative Developer & Digital Designer crafting thoughtful and visually engaging web experiences.

Hello, my name is Nande Mtshizana and I am a creative web developer and digital designer based in Johannesburg, South Africa, with a strong interest in front-end development, visual communication and user experience design. I enjoy creating digital experiences that combine clean aesthetics with thoughtful functionality, allowing users to interact with content in a way that feels intuitive, engaging and visually refined.

My work focuses on blending design and development to produce websites and digital interfaces that are both modern and meaningful. I am particularly drawn to minimal and editorial-inspired design approaches where typography, spacing, colour and structure work together to create clarity and strong visual identity. Through every project I aim to create work that feels polished, purposeful and user-centred.

I have experience working with HTML, CSS and JavaScript to build responsive and interactive websites that adapt across multiple devices and screen sizes. I enjoy experimenting with layout systems, interaction design and visual storytelling to create engaging user experiences that balance creativity with usability.

Beyond technical development, I am passionate about continuous learning and creative exploration. I value thoughtful design decisions, attention to detail and creating work that communicates ideas effectively while maintaining a strong visual presence. My portfolio reflects my interest in combining creativity, design thinking and development into cohesive digital experiences that feel both professional and expressive.
